♫ About the Visualization

This is a tree chart which shows songs that belong to different genres of music.

I decided to visualize my playlist because I wanted to see what it would look like
with everything being visualized to see what type of music I like more.
So then this means that the data points show how much the temperatures have
deviated from the standard predicted temperatures

Feel free to click through the different regions to look at the anomalies
You may also hover over the bubbles to get a much more accurate reading of the data

Data is from: https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/
